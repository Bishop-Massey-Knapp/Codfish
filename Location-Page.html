Where the hell is this barn?

We are located 4-1/2 miles Northeast of Maquoketa, Iowa on Codfish Hollow Road. 5013 288th Ave., Maquoketa, Iowa 52060

DIRECTIONS TO THE BARN:

Get to Maquoketa… From there take Highway 62 north towards Andrew (out by the Fairgrounds). Turn right about a half mile out of town onto 35th Street/Codfish Hollow Road. After about 2-1/2 miles it turns to gravel… keep going, watch out for animals. Turn left at 288th Ave.
